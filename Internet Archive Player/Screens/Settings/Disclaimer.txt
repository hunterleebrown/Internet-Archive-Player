IA Player provides access to publicly available media content from the Internet Archive (archive.org). This app is an independent client and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Internet Archive.

All content is sourced directly from Internet Archive's public collections. Content availability, quality, and accuracy are determined by Internet Archive and its contributors. This app allows users to download and store media files locally on their device for offline playback, but does not host, distribute, or modify content from the Internet Archive's servers.
